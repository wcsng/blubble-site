We really appreciate you taking the first step to make this system a reality. We are currently in the early stage of testing our algorithms and making our system trustworthy and error-free. Our current aim is to provide a robust and trustworthy classification algorithm and design an accurate risk score to each of your interactions with other people. In order to achieve this, we need to collect data from a diverse set of environments and devices.

If you have read up to here, we presume you’re definitely interested in our cause and would like to know how you could help. To this effect, we request you to take 10 minutes of your time to collect some data for us using your electronic devices. Our simple experiment will help us understand how the Bluetooth signals will change if you’re within or outside a radius of 6 feet from another device.
